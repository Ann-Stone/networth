## Notes

- Valid `type` values for `GET /reports/expenditure/{type}` must be read from api-reference.md. Common values are likely `Floating`, `Fixed`, `Invest`, `Income` — confirm before hard-coding.
- This view shares `selectedYear` with `BalanceSheetView` via the `yearReport` store. Changing the year in one view affects both.
- **Store/API mismatch (recorded in Phase 1):** `GET /reports/expenditure/{type}` takes `vesting_month: YYYYMM` (an anchor month), **not** a year. `store.fetchExpenditureReport(type, year)` anchors on `${year}12` (December of the selected year). If the backend's "anchor month" semantics mean something other than "year-end snapshot", revisit the anchor month choice or expose a month picker.
