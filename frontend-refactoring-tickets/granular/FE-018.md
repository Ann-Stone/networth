## Notes

- `GET /dashboard/alarms` is the dashboard endpoint (upcoming/today's alarms), not `GET /settings/alarms` (full CRUD list). They hit different URLs.
- Display alarms in a compact list, not a full table — this is a dashboard widget, not a management page.
- Alarm management (add/edit/delete) is in `RemindSettingView.vue` (FE-038).
