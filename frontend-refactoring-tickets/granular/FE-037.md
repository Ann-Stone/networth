## Notes

- The budget grid can be wide (13 columns: category + 12 months). Use `<el-table>` with `fixed` first column and horizontal scroll.
- `updateBudgets` is a bulk PUT — it sends the entire array, matched by `(budget_year, category_code)`. Transactional on the backend.
- `store.budgets` is `ref<BudgetRead[]>([])`. The input-number cells bind directly to `row.expected01` etc. Vue reactivity handles two-way binding.
